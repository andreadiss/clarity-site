What We Do

We support nonprofit organizations with surveys and questionnaires, project planning, and data analysis—transforming collected information into clear insights to engage donors and volunteers, create compelling fundraising campaign content, predict the success of new initiatives, demonstrate the impact of your work, and secure more funding. We provide evidence-based solutions for the nonprofit sector.

CLARITY LAB SERVICES

Stakeholders Research

Get to know your supporters more deeply

We design questionnaires and surveys to gather useful insights from volunteers, donors, and other stakeholders. We support data collection, analysis, and interpretation, delivering clear reports, infographics, and statistics you can act on.

Project Validation

Prove the impact you’re creating

We help you measure the outcomes of your project and provide concrete evidence of your impact. You'll get statistics, charts, and reports you can use to secure more funding, convince partners, and improve your interventions.

Donor-UX Starter

Improve the experience of people who want to donate online

We test your website with real users to understand what works and what doesn’t. You’ll receive a fast and practical report to optimize your digital fundraising, increase donations, reduce drop-offs, and present your organization more professionally.

Engagement Booster

Keep volunteers and supporters engaged over time

We track participation and satisfaction, identify drop‑out causes, and provide practical steps to keep enthusiasm high and grow the community around your project.

Data Guidance & Workshops

Analytics and methods always within reach

We support your data analysis and train your team to turn data into clear decisions and successful funding proposals.

Reliable Research

We apply established scientific methods to deliver accurate and repeatable results you can rely on. Our team includes researchers from top universities and R&D.

Innovative Approaches

Studying behavior and attitudes requires deep expertise in theory, soft skills, and cutting-edge technology. We use behavioral tasks, predictive analytics, and implicit measures to reveal what truly drives people.

Tailored Solutions

Every project is custom-designed for your goals: we listen to your context, analyze your audience, and guide you with clear data. We don’t offer one-size-fits-all packages; we collaborate to define key questions, choose the right tools, and interpret results practically.

Understandable Results

Data shouldn’t be intimidating. We deliver results that are easy to understand and share with your team. We translate complex numbers into clear, actionable insights, with visual reports and narratives accessible to all, even non-technical stakeholders.
