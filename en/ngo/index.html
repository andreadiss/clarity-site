What we do

We support non-profit organisations with questionnaires and surveys, project planning, and data analysis—turning the information collected into clear answers you can use to engage donors and volunteers, create fundraising content, predict the success of new campaigns, prove the effectiveness of your interventions, and secure more funding. We offer evidence-based solutions for the third sector.

CLARITY LAB SERVICES

Stakeholder Research

Understand the people who back your cause

We design questionnaires and surveys to gather useful insights from volunteers, donors, and other stakeholders. We support you in collecting, analysing, and interpreting the data, providing clear reports, infographics, and statistics.

Project Validation

Prove the change you create

We help you plan and measure your project’s results to obtain solid evidence of your impact. You’ll get statistics, charts, and reports you can use to raise more funds, convince partners, and improve your interventions.

Donor-UX Starter

Improve the experience of people who want to donate online

We test your site with real users to understand what works and what doesn’t. You get a concise, actionable report to optimise digital fundraising—boosting donations, reducing drop-offs, and giving your organisation a more professional image.

Engagement Booster

Keep volunteers and supporters engaged over time

We monitor participation and satisfaction, identify reasons for drop-out, and provide practical actions to maintain enthusiasm and grow the community around your project.

Data Guidance & Workshops

Methodology and analysis at your side

We support you with data analysis and team training, turning data into clear decisions and successful funding applications.

Reliable Research

We apply established scientific methods to deliver accurate and repeatable results you can rely on. Our team includes researchers from top universities and R&D.

Innovative Approaches

Studying behavior and attitudes requires deep expertise in theory, soft skills, and cutting-edge technology. We use behavioral tasks, predictive analytics, and implicit measures to reveal what truly drives people.

Tailored Solutions

Every project is custom-designed for your goals: we listen to your context, analyze your audience, and guide you with clear data. We don’t offer one-size-fits-all packages; we collaborate to define key questions, choose the right tools, and interpret results practically.

Understandable Results

Data shouldn’t be intimidating. We deliver results that are easy to understand and share with your team. We translate complex numbers into clear, actionable insights, with visual reports and narratives accessible to all, even non-technical stakeholders.
